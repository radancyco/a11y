Does Radancy support Accessibility Overlays?

Radancy does not support accessibility overlays or similar add-ons. More information can be found on our support page.

Is the career site WGAC compliant?

The level of compliancy can only be determined through specialized testing. This is by request only, but upon that, Radancy can comply with any WCAG 2.2 level required, typically Level AA, an industry benchmark.

Is this compliance available Out-of-the-box?

Partially. While many pre-built career site features are accessible, each site Radancy produces are custom designed and developed, so thorough testing will always be required to see where compliance stands.
