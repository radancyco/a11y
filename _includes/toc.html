Accessibility Overlays

Use of accessibility overlays are forbidden on all Radancy websites. Sites that include them can still face legal challenges because they do not address inherent accessibility barriers, do not protect one from legal challenges, and reinforce discrimination by lulling site owners into a false sense of security, who do nothing else to improve their digitasl experience. More information on why we need to avoid such solutions, can be found here:
