Accessibility Overlays

Accessibility overlays are not supported, as they are not in line with {{ site.company-name }}'s vision on how best to support people with disabilities in our digital offerings. In our experience, use of these tools may promote ableism, reinforce discrimination and are often perceived, particularly among the disabled and advocacy communities, as a performative commitment to accessibility.
