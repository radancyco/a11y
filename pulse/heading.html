--- title: Heading-Level Outline localcss: "../../pulse/init.css" ---

A web page should only have a single h1 element and avoid skipping headings. A logical heading structure is very important for everybody so that the page can be easily understood and navigated.

Note: Understand the document structure before flagging an issue. Navigation systems often contain hidden headings that the validator will present and fail due to a missing h1. However, headings in the navigation can be ignored.
